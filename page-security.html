Security of cloud printing app

Datacenter security includes physical security, requiring site planning to limit physical break-ins, and network security, where security engineers install firewalls and anti-malware programs to prevent breaches. Any business that relies on a data center for all or even part of its operations should have multiple physical and network security measures in place that protect the information contained in the data center from loss, malicious manipulation, and theft.
