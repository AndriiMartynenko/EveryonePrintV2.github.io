Product Overview

Cloud Print

Hybrid Cloud Platform (HCP) makes streamlining your print infrastructure, eliminating print servers, and incorporating cloud technologies a breeze.

Let your print infrastructure soar in the clouds with Hybrid Cloud Platform (HCP)

No print servers, amped up security, and automated IT tasks behind the scenes— featuring easy printing for everyone, from anywhere.

Ditch the unnecessary and pricey hardware

Every print server costs several thousand per year—not to mention the time suck they take to maintain. HCP is a SaaS-based solution that scales up or down with you as your needs evolve.
Serverless printing

Get rid of the costs and complexities associated with print servers—and redirect your resources to other valuable IT projects.

Control costs and easily scale with SaaS

With HCP, there’s no upfront investments. Our SaaS model gives you the flexibility you need to scale up or down. Just pay for the licenses you need.

One solution, hosted anywhere

HCP can be delivered as a hosted SaaS offering or installed as an on- premise private cloud (hence the Hybrid).

Learn more
Amp up your security and protect your data

HCP is designed for Zero Trust requirements. Protect your data with features including end-to-end server to hardware encryption, user authentication, auditing functionality, and integration with cloud-based authentication providers like Okta, Azure AD, and Google.

Learn more

Make life easy for ITand everyone printing

Printing can be a major headache for IT... whether it’s helping users get connected to a printer, updating print drivers across the organisation, or patching security holes. With HCP, printers don’t have to be such a pain.
Automate IT work

Automate those tedious print tasks like deploying new printers, provisioning drivers, and updating servers. HCP makes it easy to manage your print infrastructure from a simple web - based UI.

Multi-tenant platform

Create your own print environment on the HCP multi-tenant platform and replace existing Windows and Mac- based print infrastructures in less than 10 minutes.

Hassle-free printing for users

Users (and guests) can easily print from any device, to any location, in a matter of seconds. Users can also send their print to a queue and release jobs from the printer of their choice.

Learn more

Got a mixed fleet of printers? We don’t play favorites.

HCP offers a true single print driver that includes full finishing options for all your favorite print manufacturers. We’ve developed embedded applications that lets users print to any device—while cutting the costs and time associated with secure print deployment and ongoing management.
